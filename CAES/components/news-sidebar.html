CAES News Sidebar:

The purpose of this component is to display news articles pulled from the CAES news database.  As with the Events components, users who use this component in Adobe Experience Manger can set filters so that the only news articles that are displayed in the feed are articles that are relevant to their content. Shown below is the plain list (stacked) and tiled list (masonry tiled) version of the component. Users can slelect which display option they prefet for each instance of the component.
