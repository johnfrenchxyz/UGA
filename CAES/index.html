Orientation 2.0 5:30pm - 7:00pm| Athens, GA at Conner Hall Lobby

Enjoy free ice cream, popcorn, giveaways, and CAES t-shirts at CAES Orientation 2.0 on August 16, from 5:30-7 p.m. in the Conner Hall Lobby! Come learn about clubs, meet your fellow classmates and new professors and ask our student panel all your questions.

Aug 17
UGA Part-time Job and Internship Fair 11am = 3pm | Athens, GA at Tate Student Center, Grand Hall

Local employers on-campus and off-campus will be at the Tate Grand Hall to hire students for part-time positions and internships. This is a great opportunity to land a part-time job or internship and an opportunity to introduce yourself and give your resume directly to a hiring manager.

Aug 24
Fall Kickoff 6pm - 8pm| Athens, GA at Four Towers Lawn

All CAES Students are invited to attend the Fall Kickoff on August 24th at 6 p.m. sponsored by the CAES Alumni Association! Come to Four Towers lawn to enjoy a free dinner, live music, games, and prizes. This is also a great chance to get to know other students and explore all our clubs!
