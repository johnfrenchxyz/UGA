September 8, 2015

Meet Your CAES Academic Counselor: Jennifer Merrifield

Did you know that CAES is one of only two colleges at UGA to have its own Academic Counselor? For the past few years Jennifer Merrifield has served in this role and has been active in helping students interact with personal issues in such a way that they find success inside and outside the classroom. Academic Counseling is a free service offered to any student who seeks aid in coping with major transitions throughout their college years. Learn more about Jennifer on our website!

CAES Kudos

CAES's very own Erin Burnett is featured this week as UGA's Amazing Student! Erin is not only an Agricultural Communications major who will graduate in May but is the face behind the CAES Students' Twitter, Facebook, and Instagram! Take a moment to learn more about Erin and congratulate her today!

AgDawgs Abroad

CAES is teaming up with Heifer International to create digital storytelling projects in two Romanian communities. Students will use photography, video and written narrative to capture the essence of local agriculture in Romania. Read more about this study abroad program here.
