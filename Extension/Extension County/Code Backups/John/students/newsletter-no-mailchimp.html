Explore, Discover and Learn on a CAES Study Abroad Program

Did you know that CAES has 10 study abroad programs from which to choose? Studying abroad is a life-changing adventure which gives you hands-on cultural learning and a meaningful academic experience. On any CAES study abroad program, you will gain a fresh perspective on people and the world, make new friends, eat exotic foods and create lasting memories – all while earning academic credit toward your degree. Worried about money? Scholarships are available to CAES students! If you’re not sure which program is right for you or for more information on any study abroad programs or scholarships, contact Amanda Stephens to schedule an advising appointment. 

Club Deadlines

Today, September 15th, is the deadline for registering clubs with the Center for Student Organizations! All clubs affiliated with CAES must go through this process in order to receive funding. Clubs are a fantastic way to network with peers with the same interests and with companies in your field so make sure your club has everything it needs to begin another great year at UGA! Learn more here.

CAES Kudos

This July, Kayla Alward, a Dairy Science student in CAES, won the American Dairy Science Association Undergraduate Paper Presentation. This is a huge honor from a well-known organization! Read more about Kayla and her accomplishments. Congratulations, Kayla!
