Dear CAES Students!

As the semester quickly comes to an end with finals and end of the year projects, I want to wish all our students well with their academics. To our seniors, who will be finishing up their undergraduate experience and venturing on to careers, graduate school or professional programs, we are appreciative of your time with us. I hope the experiences we provided through CAES classrooms, study abroad, undergraduate research, or extracurricular activities afforded you an opportunity of personal growth and preparedness.

Seniors, we are frequently asked by prospective students and their parents, as well as many others, about the career paths of our graduates. Having this information greatly helps us to promote our college. We would very much appreciate your response to a short survey to tell us about your career plans, even if your plans are not finalized. This survey will only take five minutes of your time, and the data we collect is COMPLETELY CONFIDENTIAL (all information you provide will be published in aggregate form only). Thank you for helping us collect this important information.

Congratulations seniors and we look forward to seeing you at the upcoming commencement activities.

Please use the following link to complete the survey:
http://career.uga.edu/gradsurvey

-Dean J. Scott Angle
